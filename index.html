Frequently Asked Questions

What is Halo?

Halo is a digital guardian angel for focus—an app that protects your time, blocks distractions, and helps you stay committed to the person you're becoming.

How does Halo help me stay focused?

Halo uses intelligent zone-based focus modes and community accountability to help you maintain focus. It gently guides you away from distractions and keeps you on track with your goals.

Does Halo block apps or notifications?

Halo works alongside your device's built-in features to help you manage screen time and notifications. It provides context-aware suggestions to protect your focus without being overly restrictive.

What makes Halo different from built-in screen time features?

Unlike basic screen time trackers, Halo combines location-based zones, community support, and personalized insights to create a holistic focus experience that adapts to your life.

Is Halo free to use?

Halo offers a free tier with essential focus features. Premium features are available for users who want to unlock the full power of their digital guardian angel.
